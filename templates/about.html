About This Project

Purpose

The India Renewable Manufacturing Tracker is an automated system designed to monitor and catalog solar and battery manufacturing projects across India. As the country rapidly expands its renewable energy capacity, keeping track of manufacturing capabilities is crucial for stakeholders in the industry.

Features

  • Automated scraping of news sources for project updates
  • Intelligent extraction of project details from articles
  • Comprehensive tracking of both solar and battery manufacturing facilities
  • Detailed project profiles with technical specifications and investment information
  • Excel export functionality for data analysis
  • Regular scheduled updates (twice daily)

Technical Information

This tracker is built using Python and Flask with a SQLite database backend. It uses web scraping libraries (BeautifulSoup, newspaper3k, trafilatura) to gather information from reputable news sources covering the renewable energy sector in India. The system employs natural language processing techniques to extract relevant project details and maintain an organized database.

Tracked Data Points

For each project, we track:

  • Project name and company
  • Location information
  • Manufacturing capacity
  • Project status and timeline
  • Investment details
  • PLI scheme status
  • Input/output materials
  • Approvals and clearances

Why Track Manufacturing?

India has set ambitious renewable energy goals, targeting 500 GW of non-fossil fuel capacity by 2030. Achieving these goals requires not just project deployment but also a robust domestic manufacturing ecosystem. This tracker helps:

Industry Analysts

Track manufacturing capacity growth and identify market trends

Project Developers

Identify potential equipment suppliers and evaluate their capabilities

Policy Makers

Assess the effectiveness of manufacturing incentives and policies

Investors

Identify investment opportunities and track market competition
